Plan Your Stay

Less planning. More mountain time.

Three simple itineraries to help families and couples make the most of a Smokies getaway.

Three-day family escape

A balanced first visit.

DAY 1

Arrive & settle in

Stock up near town, check in after 4 PM, explore the cabin, make dinner, and enjoy the hot tub.

DAY 2

Dollywood day

Leave early, spend the day at Dollywood, then return for a quiet deck dinner and fireside evening.

DAY 3

National Park & Gatlinburg

Take a scenic drive or easy hike in the morning, then visit Gatlinburg before returning to the cabin.

Rainy-day plan

Ripley’s Aquarium, WonderWorks, local dining, arcade time in the loft, and a cozy movie night.

Couples’ reset

Slow breakfast, scenic drive, a quiet lunch, sunset views, hot tub, and fireside drinks.

Outdoor weekend

Roaring Fork, waterfall trails, picnic stops, Cades Cove, and restorative evenings at the cabin.

Good to know

Mountain-smart preparation.

Check weather before driving, allow extra time during peak seasons, download maps where service may be limited, and consider travel insurance for unexpected disruptions.

Check-in begins at 4 PM. Checkout is by 10 AM. A personalized door code is sent before arrival.

Quick packing list

Comfortable walking shoes
Weather layers
Swimsuits for the hot tub/pool
Refillable water bottles
Groceries for the first evening
Any specialty toiletries or child supplies
