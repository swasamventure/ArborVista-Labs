FAQ & House Rules

Clear expectations make better stays.

Key information about the layout, arrival, parking, resort access, and booking policies.

How many guests can the cabin accommodate?

The cabin sleeps six: one king bed, one queen bed, and two twin beds in the open upper loft.

Is the loft a private third bedroom?

No. It is an open loft overlooking the main living space and is best suited for children or teens.

How many bathrooms are there?

There are 2.5 bathrooms: two full ensuite bathrooms and one main-level half bathroom.

What are check-in and checkout times?

Check-in begins at 4:00 PM and checkout is by 10:00 AM.

How does entry work?

A personalized smart-lock code is sent three days before arrival. There is no office stop or key pickup.

Is parking available?

Yes. Free off-street parking is available for approximately 2–3 vehicles.

Are pets allowed?

No. Arbor Vista Retreat is strictly pet-free. Violations may result in a $500 fee, immediate removal, and no refund.

Is smoking or vaping allowed?

No smoking or vaping is permitted inside. Violations may result in a $2,000 cleaning and deodorizing fee.

Is there a minimum booking age?

Yes. The primary guest must be at least 23 years old.

What resort amenities are included?

Seasonal pool, pickleball, tennis, basketball, playground, frisbee golf, cornhole, fitness trail, picnic pavilion, and gated entrance, subject to resort rules and availability.

Is a rental agreement required?

Yes. A signed electronic rental agreement is required after booking.
