Frequently asked questions.

What time is check-in?

Check-in begins at 4:00 PM. Door access activates at that time.

What time is checkout?

Checkout is by 10:00 AM.

How many guests can stay?

Arbor Vista Retreat sleeps up to six guests.

Are pets allowed?

No pets unless approved in writing. Service-animal requests are handled as required by applicable law.

How does Shagbark gate access work?

Guests submit driver name, email, mobile number and vehicle count. The resort sends a registration code for the guard gate.
