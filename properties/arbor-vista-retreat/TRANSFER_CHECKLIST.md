# Property Transfer Checklist

This folder defines the property-specific package that can be transferred with a future sale.

Transferable assets:
- Domain and DNS records
- Public website repository
- Property configuration
- Property photos and content
- Guest guidebook and house rules
- Property-specific iCal endpoints
- Future reservations when permitted by the purchase agreement

Do not automatically transfer historical guest personal data, payment records, or unrelated portfolio data.
Export and transfer those only after legal/privacy review.
