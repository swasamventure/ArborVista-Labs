Packing made easy

Provided at the cabin

Bed linens, bath and hot-tub towels, pots, pans, dishes, utensils, hand soap, dishwasher pods and starter quantities of paper products and toiletries.

Starter supplies may not cover an entire longer stay. Bring groceries plus preferred oil, foil, zip bags, sprays, condiments and specialty items.

Mountain awareness

Protect guests and wildlife.

Bear safety

Remove food, drinks, coolers and scented items from vehicles. Lock vehicles and cabin doors.

Trash

Bag all trash and place it inside the bear-proof exterior bin. Never leave loose trash outside.

Climate

Keep exterior doors closed, especially in summer, so the cabin HVAC can maintain comfort.
